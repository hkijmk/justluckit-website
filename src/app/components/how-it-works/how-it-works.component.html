How It Works

Go to the “Play” Screen

Press the “Play” button to go to the play screen.

Connect Your Wallet

Connect your wallet using the “Connect Wallet” button, and select your wallet. To learn how to connect your wallet, check our FAQ section.

Play Your Numbers

Choose six numbers and press “Play”. Follow the confirmation dialogs to finish the transaction.

Claim Reward

Once the “Draw Results” are announced, check your coupons from “My Coupons”. Press the “Claim Reward” button on the winner coupons or “Get Deposit” on the others to get your deposit back. Learn more…
